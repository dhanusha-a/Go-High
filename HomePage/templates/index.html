About Us

Our early childhood was shaped by the beauty of the local mountains. A passion developed over the course of the years from this fascination, which remains undiminished to this day. To share the uniqueness of this landscape with other people has always been, and will always be of great importance to me. And so, with the founding of Mountain Sports not only did our dreams come true, we also found our true calling for a career.
For over 20 years, Mountain Sports in Leh - Ladakh has been well known to nature lovers and adventurers far beyond the borders of the region for its exceptional, diversified adventure program.

When you plan a trip to the Smoky Mountains, you’ll quickly find there’s something for everyone to do. Taking friends or family, or with the group planned by the our community, our itineraries will help you plan your trip to match your vacation style. We look for every detail to make your trips memorable and of course, safe.

Trainings

Learn from the best and stay above the rest !

All of our nationally-recognised schemes are designed to educate and train people so that they can safely enjoy the climbing walls, crags, coast, hills and mountains of our beautiful country. Our qualifications train and assess people to look after other people in these environments, whether that's leading a walk in the mountains, coaching in a climbing wall or teaching multi pitch rock climbing. People who hold our qualifications have been assessed at a nationally agreed standard and may be volunteers, youth workers, outdoor instructors or teachers.
